What is Community Hades Appreciation Day?

An opportunity for all of us, as a group of people who enjoy playing Hades' Star, to stop for a moment and show that we this game and the people who created it. For some of us, it's been part of our lives for years. Whether you've been playing Hades for a short time or many years, it is unique as a game and deserves recognition for that.

Why is the name sort of awkward? It's hard to say.

Because Community Hades Appreciation Day is abbreviated CHAD. And let's face it, we all want to be Community Hades Appreciation Displayers (CHADs).

An example of a male CHAD. Female CHADs will of course decide which is their best side and imagine a perfect photo taken from that side.

How can I participate?

If you read this far, good for you!

  1. Spread the word. We all need more CHADs. GC, social media, however you like, getinvolved!
  2. Send Andreas a positive message (click here) for creating something you enjoy.
  3. If you don't usually, buy a weekly crystal pack. Developers need to be paid too. If you usually do (nice one!), carry on, as you were.
  4. Update (or add!) your review for Hades' Star - more players is good for everyone and helps keep the game prominent in the app stores ( ) ( ) ( ).
  5. Tweet about it! Add @HadesStarGame to your tweets, and use #hadesStarGame as a hash tag to help new players get into the game.
